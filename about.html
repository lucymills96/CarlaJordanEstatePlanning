About Us

Define your legacy with clarity, we’ll take care of the details.

At Carla Jordan Estate Planning, we understand that estate planning is about more than just legal documents, it’s about protecting your loved ones, honouring your wishes, and securing peace of mind for the future.

We believe in making the process clear, compassionate, and personal. From your first consultation to the final documents, every stage is handled with care, clarity, and discretion. Our thoughtful, client-focused approach ensures that what matters most to you is understood and safeguarded.

Whether you're creating your first Will, setting up a Lasting Power of Attorney, or planning for future generations, we take the time to understand your unique circumstances and provide practical, legally sound solutions tailored to your values.

Our focus is on building long lasting relationships and delivering peace of mind, so you can feel confident knowing your affairs are in safe hands.

Meet Carla

Carla brings over six years of legal experience and a warm, client-focused approach to every interaction. She has built a strong foundation in law and is known for her ability to make complex matters easy to understand.

To expand her expertise and support clients in a new capacity, Carla completed specialist training in will writing and estate planning, achieving full membership with The Society of Will Writers. As an accredited member, she adheres to a strict Code of Practice and completes regular continuing professional development to stay current with changes in the law.

Carla takes pride in helping people make informed and confident decisions about their future. Whether you're planning ahead or navigating sensitive family circumstances, she offers personalised support rooted in trust, empathy, and meticulous attention to detail.
