Letter of Wishes

Guiding your trustees and expressing your voice

What is a letter of wishes?

A Letter of Wishes is a document that offers valuable and clear guidance to your trustees or executors about how you would like your estate to be handled, or how certain assets should be managed or distributed in line with your personal values and intentions.

A Letter of Wishes can include explanations for specific decisions, personal messages to loved ones, or guidance on how you’d like your children to be raised if guardians have been appointed. It adds a meaningful, human touch to your formal arrangements, helping your loved ones feel supported and more connected to your wishes. Importantly, it can be updated at any time without needing to amend your Will, offering flexibility and peace of mind as your circumstances change.
